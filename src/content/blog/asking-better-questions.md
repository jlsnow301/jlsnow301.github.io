---
title: Asking better questions
description: Growth gets easier when ego stops guarding every gap in knowledge.
date: 2023-06-01
tag: learning
---

One of the easiest ways to get stuck in software is to let ego decide which questions are safe to ask. People stay quiet because they do not want to sound uninformed about some acronym, tool, pattern, or system they think everyone else already understands. But this field is far too large for anyone to know all of it. The better habit is to ask the stupid question anyway, whether that means asking what a service does, what a term means, why a team made a choice, or what changed. The question that feels embarrassing is usually the one that clears the most confusion, exposes hidden assumptions, and saves more time than silence ever does.
