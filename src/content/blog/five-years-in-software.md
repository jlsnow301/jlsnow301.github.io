---
title: Reflections on five years in software
description: Learning from different teams, projects, and self-directed work.
date: 2023-05-15
tag: career
---

Experience matters, but it does not all look the same. Working on a team
teaches one set of lessons, while independent projects teach a very different
one.

A lot of growth comes from building without a complete map. You make decisions,
live with tradeoffs, and learn how to recover when the first pass misses the
mark.

Different teams, managers, and project styles have helped keep my perspective
wide. Continuous learning still feels like the center of the job.
