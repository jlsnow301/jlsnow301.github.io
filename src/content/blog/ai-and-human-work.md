---
title: AI and human work
description: Why new tools change software practice without replacing human judgment.
date: 2023-06-15
tag: ai
---

AI tools are changing software work quickly. Repetitive tasks are getting
cheaper, and things like drafting, search, and first-pass implementation move
faster than they used to.

That does not change the need for human judgment. Product sense, tradeoffs,
editing, and responsibility still belong to people.

The useful response is neither fear nor hype. Use the tools well, keep your
standards high, and keep your craft sharp.
